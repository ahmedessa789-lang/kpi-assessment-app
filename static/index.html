About App

Reap Service KPI Dashboard

This application helps management review business performance through KPI analysis, financial statement review, automated Excel import, visual charts, and executive reporting in one place.

The dashboard is designed so each module supports a different business need: CEO performance review, finance analysis, AI recommendations, and consolidated reporting.

CEO Module

Track commercial and operational KPIs, import executive Excel files, run AI-based analysis, and monitor overall business health from one executive screen.

Finance Module

Import Excel files, calculate financial statements, review mapping, and analyze the company’s financial position clearly.

Analytics Module

View KPI charts, recommendations, strongest areas, weakest areas, and trend signals to support management decisions.

Reports Module

Prepare structured reports with metrics, summaries, tables, and later full charts and executive outputs for presentations and review.

The goal of this application is to give management a practical business intelligence tool that is easy to use, visually clear, and ready for presentation.
